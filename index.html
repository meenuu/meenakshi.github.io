Project 01 · Featured
Met Museum Personal Art Tour Generator
An AI that learns your art taste from 20 ratings and builds a personalised gallery-by-gallery tour of the Metropolitan Museum of Art — ranked by predicted enjoyment with SHAP explanations on every recommendation. Built a scraper against the Met's public API, engineered 315 features per artwork (TF-IDF + one-hot encoding + era bucketing), trains a Random Forest in real time, and outputs a full walking roadmap. 12 hardcoded must-see masterpieces — Van Gogh, Rembrandt, Vermeer, Monet, Pollock — guaranteed in every tour.
